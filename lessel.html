When early analog over dubbing casette recorders became affordable, Bob and Lon began recording a series of original songs. Bob wrote all lyrics and melodies, Lon the music beds. Instruments varied over the years and included Fender Rhodes, Roland, Yamaha and various other small keyboards, some MIDI, saxophone, flute, vibes and many percussion instruments.
